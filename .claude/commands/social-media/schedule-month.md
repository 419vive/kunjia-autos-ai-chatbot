# 30-Day Content Calendar

Create a full month of social media content, generate image prompts, write captions, and schedule via Blotato.

## Instructions

1. Read `.claude/skills/blotato-social-media/SKILL.md` for platform rules
2. Read `brand.md` for brand voice and content pillars
3. Plan 30 unique posts with a mix of content types (educational, inspirational, promotional, social proof)
4. For each post: write the hook, caption (per platform), and an image generation prompt
5. Schedule all 30 posts through Blotato MCP, one per day starting tomorrow at 9am
6. Output a summary calendar table showing date, content type, hook, and platforms
